About Rob

Creator, Strategist
& Entrepreneur

Rob Clark is a creator, strategist, and entrepreneur focused on attention, storytelling, and growth in a distracted world. He is best known as the founder and face of That Tall Family, one of the largest family-friendly brands on social media — generating over 5 billion views and building an audience of more than 4 million followers across TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and Facebook through organic content only.

Professionally, Rob helps creators, businesses, colleges, churches, and nonprofits clarify their message and turn social media into real visibility, trust, and opportunity through consulting, speaking, content strategy, and hands-on execution under TTF Media.
