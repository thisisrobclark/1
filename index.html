Speaker, Strategist,
Author & Creator

Rob Clark is a speaker, strategist, author, and creator who has built one of the most successful organic social media brands in the world. As the founder of That Tall Family, Rob and his family have generated over 5 billion views and built a following of more than 4 million people across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, and LinkedIn without relying on paid ads.

His work sits at the intersection of attention, storytelling, leadership, and culture. Rob has worked with brands, universities, nonprofits, and entrepreneurs who know they need to be visible but lack a clear strategy for how attention actually works today. His talks are practical, direct, and grounded in real world experience, not trends or tactics that expire in six months. Rob lives just south of Nashville, Tennessee, and speaks nationally.

What Rob Speaks About

Rob brings practical, proven strategies from building one of the largest family brands on social media. No theory — just what actually works.

01
Attention Economy

Winning Attention in a Noisy World

We live in an attention economy where visibility is survival. In this keynote, Rob shares how he built one of the most-watched family brands in the world without spending a dime on advertising—and how your organization can do the same. This talk cuts through the noise about algorithms and trends and delivers a clear framework for earning real attention.

Key Takeaways:

  • Why attention is the new currency and how to earn it organically
  • The framework Rob uses to create content that resonates with millions
  • How to stop chasing trends and start building lasting visibility

02
Higher Ed & Nonprofits

Closing the Attention Gap

Great institutions often have an attention problem. They do incredible work but struggle to be seen in a crowded digital landscape. This keynote is designed for colleges, universities, and nonprofits who want to stop being invisible and start winning the attention they deserve. Rob shares a practical roadmap for building visibility without compromising values.

Key Takeaways:

  • Why traditional marketing no longer reaches prospective students and donors
  • How to tell your institution's story in a way that cuts through the noise
  • A content strategy that works without a massive budget or team

03
Faith & Culture

Faith, Media & the Modern World

Churches, ministries, and faith-based organizations face a unique challenge: how do you engage a rapidly changing culture without losing your convictions? Rob shares how he integrates faith and family into a brand seen by billions—and offers a practical framework for faith-based organizations to engage the modern world with clarity and conviction.

Key Takeaways:

  • How to engage culture authentically without compromising your message
  • The role of storytelling in sharing faith in a skeptical age
  • Practical steps for building a digital presence that reflects your mission

04
Leadership & Storytelling

Leading with Story

The best leaders know that data informs, but stories move people. In this keynote, Rob unpacks the power of narrative in leadership and shows how the principles of attention and storytelling apply far beyond social media. Whether you're leading a team, a company, or a movement, this talk will transform how you communicate and connect.

Key Takeaways:

  • Why story is the most powerful leadership tool you have
  • How to craft a narrative that inspires action and loyalty
  • The connection between personal brand and organizational impact
