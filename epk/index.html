About

About Rob Clark

Rob Clark is a speaker, strategist, author, and creator who has built one of the most successful organic social media brands in the world. As the founder of That Tall Family, Rob and his family have generated over 5 billion views and built a following of more than 4 million people across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, and LinkedIn without relying on paid ads.


His work sits at the intersection of attention, storytelling, leadership, and culture. Rob has worked with brands, universities, nonprofits, and entrepreneurs who know they need to be visible but lack a clear strategy for how attention actually works today.


His talks are practical, direct, and grounded in real world experience, not trends or tactics that expire in six months. Rob lives just south of Nashville, Tennessee, and speaks nationally.

Keynotes

Signature Keynotes

Each keynote is designed to inspire and equip your audience with actionable insights.

The Most Important Skill Every Brand Needs Now: Earning Attention

Attention is no longer guaranteed. It must be earned. In this keynote, Rob breaks down how attention actually works today, why most organizations are invisible without realizing it, and how to build relevance without selling your soul or chasing trends.

Key Takeaways

  • How attention has shifted and why traditional marketing is failing
  • Why short form content drives long term trust
  • How to communicate clearly in a noisy world
  • What actually converts views into influence

From Views to Value: Turning Attention Into Real Growth

Millions of views mean nothing if they do not lead to action. Rob walks audiences through how to turn attention into trust, trust into momentum, and momentum into measurable outcomes.

Key Takeaways

  • Why most content fails to convert
  • How to build credibility at scale
  • What real growth looks like beyond likes and followers
  • How to move people from watching to acting

Made for Influence: Showing Up Without Apology

This talk is for leaders, believers, and builders who feel called to influence culture but feel pressure to shrink back. Rob shares his personal journey and challenges audiences to stop waiting for permission to show up with clarity and courage.

Key Takeaways

  • Why influence is a responsibility not an ego play
  • How faith and visibility can coexist
  • What obedience looks like in a public world
  • How to lead with conviction instead of fear

Attention Gap: How Colleges and Institutions Win in a Distracted World

Higher education and nonprofits are facing an attention crisis. Rob helps institutions understand why posting more is not the answer and what actually creates relevance with the next generation.

Key Takeaways

  • Why most institutional social media fails
  • How to build trust with Gen Z and Millennials
  • What content actually earns attention for colleges
  • How to align mission with modern communication
